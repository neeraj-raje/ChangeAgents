What Twelve Months of Deliberate Visibility Looks Like

A realistic, milestone-based view of what to expect — and when.

90 Days
Foundation

Clarity, Setup, and Early Traction

The first three months build the foundation. You leave with a positioning strategy, a rebuilt LinkedIn presence, and your first thought leadership in the market.

AreaWhere You Are
PositioningNarrative and themes documented
LinkedInFully optimised profile live
Content8–12 thought leadership posts published

MediaFirst conversations initiated with journalists

Peers respond to your posts. Industry contacts reference things you have published.

6 Months
Recognition

Authority Building and Ecosystem Presence

Your name is circulating in industry conversations. You have been quoted, on a panel or podcast, and the right people recognise your perspective.

AreaWhere You Are
Content25–40 thought leadership pieces published
AudienceNoticeable growth in relevant professional followers
Speaking1–3 engagements secured
MediaQuotes or features in industry publications

Conference organisers know your name. Peers share your content.

12 Months
Influence

Recognised as an Industry Voice

A year of consistent visibility produces compounding results — a content library, an engaged audience, and a track record that opens doors that were previously closed.

AreaWhere You Are
Content60–100 pieces of thought leadership created
Speaking3–6 conference or panel appearances
MediaRegular commentary opportunities with industry publications
PlatformEstablished newsletter, podcast, or content series
RecognitionNominations or listings in industry rankings

You are invited to speak before you apply. Analysts reference your perspectives.

Professional milestone visual. Career arc or industry recognition. Abstract or conceptual acceptable.

18 to 24 Months — True Industry Authority

These are the signals that indicate genuine, durable influence.

  • Invitations to keynote industry conferences
  • Invitations to serve on industry advisory boards
  • Proactive requests for media commentary on major technology developments
  • Analysts citing your perspectives in published research
  • Posts that change the direction of industry conversation

These milestones represent realistic outcomes from a consistent, well-executed programme. Individual results vary. We do not promise specific numbers — we promise systematic progress.
